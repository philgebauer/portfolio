E and T Designs

This was a fun project for a client who wanted a super simple site to showcase their deisgns and prints that they normally sell over on Etsy. The website needed to be fast and responsive - looking great on a laptop, tablet or even your phone.

The site layout, design and logo were all done with the goal of simplicity in mind. I wanted the site to match their simple, quirky and playful designs.



Technology Used

This was created using Bootstrap, CSS3, HTML5, and jQuery. Hosted and deployed on Heroku using Git as version control.
